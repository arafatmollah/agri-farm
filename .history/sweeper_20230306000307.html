Orkan

The ORKAN municipal sweeper, thanks to the universal mounting, can be used as a front or rear sweeper. The swinging attachment allows the sweeper to adapt to uneven terrain, which allows the brush to pick up dirt from the corners of the road. An additional side brush with adjustable height and tilt perfectly collects dirt from under curbs, sweeping it under the main brush. The electric sprinkler reduces dust during cleanup by spraying the collected material with water. The sweeper with full hydraulics allows full control from the tractor cab without leaving and manual adjustment.
STANDARD EQUIPMENT:
- waste tank - hydraulically controlled
- mechanical adjustment of the working angle of the main brush
- three-point linkage mounting (at the front or rear of the tractor)
- pumped wheels
- changing the direction of rotation of the main brush
- hydrostatic drive
- brushes made of synthetic bristles reinforced with steel wire
